Salsa

Salsa is a popular form of social dance that originated in New York City with strong influences from:

  • Latin America
  • particularly Puerto Rico
  • Cuba

The movements of salsa have origins in

  1. Cuban Son,
  2. Cha-cha-cha,
  3. Mambo
  4. and others

and the dance, along with the salsa music, originated in the mid-1970s in New York.
